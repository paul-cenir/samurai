Did you know

New samurai swords were tested on prisoners - live human beings!

The point of a samurai sword is called the kissaki. It is the hardest part to forge and often determines the overall quality.

Samurai gave their swords names as they believed the sword contained their warrior spirit.

Darth Vader's costume in Star Wars was inspired by samurai armour.

Samurai in popular culture

In Movies

Released in 2003, the Last Samurai, starring Tom Cruise and Ken Watanabe is the story of an American army captain and a samurai warrior at a time when the Japanese emperor is looking towards Westernisation. The Emperor employs Algren to train his army and to help eradicate the samurai. But Algen soon finds his heart is with the enemy. Akira Kurosawa's The Seven Samurai (1954)is considered one of the most influential films ever made. It still tops polls and is one of the few Japansese films well known in the West. It follows the story of a village of farmers that hires seven masterless samurai (ronin) to combat bandits intending to steal their crops.
